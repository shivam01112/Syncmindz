About Founder

Gaurav Kapoor: Over a distinguished 20-year career, Gaurav Kapoor has navigated dynamic industries, showcasing his versatility from the telecom sector to organized retail. Recognized as a startup expert, Kapoor's key talents lie in leadership, robust business acumen, strategic thinking, and a proven track record in franchise acquisition and retail footprints expansion.

A pivotal achievement in Kapoor's trajectory was his remarkable contribution to Lenskart, where he spearheaded the acquisition of franchisees and played a key role in the opening of over 1000 stores. His strategic vision and hands-on approach were instrumental in expanding Lenskart's retail footprint, solidifying his reputation as a luminary in the organized retail landscape.

As the Business Development Head for North & West India at Lenskart, Kapoor orchestrated strategic initiatives that not only propelled Lenskart's growth but also strategically aligned the retail businesses of Vision Express, Centre for Sight, and John Jacobs. His multifaceted role showcased his ability to drive synergies and unlock value across diverse brands within the eyewear sector.

Kapoor's leadership journey also took him to the role of Vice President at the renowned eye hospital chain, Centre for Sight. Here, he played a pivotal role in shaping the retail strategy, contributing to the overall success of the organization.

Transitioning to the Aditya Birla Group, Kapoor assumed the role of Territory Head at More Super Markets. His leadership acumen was crucial in turning around the financial trajectory of More Super Markets, transforming previously loss-making businesses into profitable ventures, a testament to his financial and operational expertise.

During his tenure with Reliance Vision Express, Kapoor demonstrated his capability to revive and transform businesses. He applied his strategic insights to make Reliance Vision Express a profitable venture, showcasing his resilience and ability to implement effective business strategies.

In the telecom sector, Kapoor's success was evident in the Tata Docomo venture of Tata Teleservices. By strategically appointing distributors and key retailers, he played a vital role in making the telecom venture a success, further highlighting his proficiency in operations and strategic management.

In conclusion, Gaurav Kapoor's journey reflects a dynamic professional who seamlessly transitions between industries, excelling in leadership, franchise acquisition, retail property acquisition, and retail operations. His strategic foresight, coupled with hands-on execution, has left an indelible mark on the success stories of Lenskart, Centre for Sight, More Super Markets, and Tata Docomo, making him a true luminary in the realms of organized retail and telecommunications.

Gaurav Mittal: Gaurav Mittal, with a career spanning 15 years, has carved a niche for himself as a specialist in retail store acquisition for both national and regional brands. His journey commenced with City Bank, where he embarked on a pivotal role in property acquisition, setting the stage for a career marked by strategic vision and impactful contributions.

In the early stages of Mittal's career, his role at City Bank involved navigating the intricate landscape of property acquisition. His sharp acumen and attention to detail laid the groundwork for his subsequent ventures, showcasing a foresight that would become a hallmark of his career.

One of the significant chapters in Mittal's professional journey unfolded when he played an instrumental role in the openings of more superstores, backed by the prestigious Aditya Birla Group. This experience not only underscored his ability to collaborate with major industry players but also demonstrated his knack for contributing to the growth of national retail chains.

Anecdotes from Mittal's career highlight his hands-on involvement in scouting and securing prime locations for retail outlets. His strategic approach went beyond the conventional, ensuring that each store acquisition aligned with the brand's identity and catered to the unique demands of the local market.

Mittal's success in the retail sector is a testament to his adaptability and resilience. His ability to identify opportunities for growth, coupled with a deep understanding of the retail landscape, has positioned him as a key player in the field. The anecdotes from his journey narrate a story of navigating challenges and leveraging opportunities, showcasing Mittal's commitment to excellence in every aspect of retail store acquisition.

In essence, Gaurav Mittal's career trajectory is a testament to his proficiency in retail store acquisition and his ability to contribute significantly to the expansion of major retail brands. His journey is not just a chronicle of professional growth but a narrative of strategic foresight and impactful contributions to the dynamic world of retail
