About Us

Syncmindz Private Limited, founded by Gaurav Mittal and Gaurav Kapoor, is a forward-thinking real estate consultancy specializing in leasing, sale, and purchase of commercial and retail properties. With both founders bringing rich and extensive experience in retail real estate leasing, the company has established itself as a trusted partner for fast-paced retail expansion across India.

Syncmindz combines deep industry understanding with AI-driven market intelligence to identify high-potential locations, optimize property selection, and accelerate brand growth.

What We Offer

Retail & Commercial Leasing

We focus on retail and commercial leasing, strategically connecting brands with high-impact locations that boost visibility and customer engagement. Backed by detailed market insights and expert negotiation, we ensure our clients achieve optimal site selection, long-term growth, and superior ROI.

Property Sale & Purchase

At Syncmindz, we facilitate seamless sale and purchase transactions across commercial, retail, and residential properties. Backed by deep market understanding and real estate expertise, our team manages every stage — from valuation and due diligence to negotiation and closure — with utmost transparency and precision. We enable our clients to make well-informed decisions and maximize the long-term value of their assets.

Warehousing & Industrial Solutions

We offer customized leasing and purchase solutions for warehousing and industrial spaces across major logistics hubs. Leveraging our extensive network and data-driven insights, we help businesses enhance supply chain efficiency, reduce operational costs, and secure strategically located assets that enable scalable growth.

Residential Leasing & Investment Advisory

We provide end-to-end residential leasing and investment advisory services for both individual and institutional clients. From luxury apartments to builder floors and long-term rentals, we curate properties that perfectly align with our clients’ lifestyle aspirations, investment objectives, and portfolio growth goals.
